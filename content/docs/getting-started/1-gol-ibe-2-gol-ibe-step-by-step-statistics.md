# Statistics

The Statistics section of the GOL IBE admin console gives you an overview of your agency's booking activity and revenue. You can view, filter, and export booking data to analyze performance.

## What you can find in Statistics

- Overview of all bookings made through your GOL IBE
- Booking counts, revenue, and ticket prices
- Filtering by date range, dealer, carrier, destination, and more
- Export options for detailed reporting
