# Statistics and sales data

The Statistics section in the GOL IBE admin console lets you view and export your booking data.

## How to access Statistics

1. Log into the admin console.
2. Go to **Statistics**.

## What data is available

- All bookings created through your GOL IBE front-end
- Date of booking, departure, carrier, route, and price
- Revenue totals and booking counts
- Filtering by date range, dealer, destination, and carrier

## How to export booking data

1. In the **Statistics** section, set the date range and any other filters you need.
2. Click **Export** (available as CSV or Excel format).
3. The file downloads to your computer and can be opened in Excel or any spreadsheet tool.

## Tips

- Export monthly reports regularly to track revenue trends.
- Filter by **Dealer** to compare performance across your dealers.
- Use the date filters to focus on specific travel periods or booking windows.
