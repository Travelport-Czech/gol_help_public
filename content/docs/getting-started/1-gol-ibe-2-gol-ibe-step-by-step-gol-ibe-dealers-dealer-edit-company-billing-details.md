# Company billing details

<!-- tags: billing details, company, invoice, VAT, dealer, optional -->

You may manage this section in the admin console in section **Dealer - Edit - Billing information**.

The fields use the following visibility options:

- **Optional** (facultative) — the field is shown but not required
- **Required** (mandatory) — the field must be filled in
- **Hidden** — the field will not be shown to the customer
