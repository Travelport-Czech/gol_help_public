# Notification templates

## How to access Notification templates

1. Log into the admin console.
2. Go to **Notifications → Notification templates**.

You will find all notification templates in this section. For each template you can:

- **EDIT** — modify the way your notification looks: text, colors, logo, etc.
- **DELIVERY** — specify which users should receive that notification.
- **COPY** — create a copy of an existing template.
- **DELETE** — remove the template (not recommended).

## Most used notifications

- **GOL IBE - Registration** — sent to the person who registered in your GOL IBE account. They become a registered customer.
- **GOL IBE - Forgotten password** — sent when a client clicks "Forgotten password" on your GOL IBE front-end, allowing them to set a new password.
- **Air tickets - Booking created without attachment and PNR** — confirmation sent to the client whenever a booking is made.
- **Air tickets - Booking created with attachment and PNR** — sent to the agency and dealers whenever a booking is made.
- **Air tickets - Booking canceled** — confirmation sent to the client when a booking is canceled.
- **Air tickets - Ticket issued** — confirmation including the flight ticket.
- **Payments - Booking paid** — sent to the client when a credit card payment is successful.
- **Payments - Repeated payment** — sent when a customer pays accidentally twice.
- Notifications whose subject starts with "Travelfusion" relate to Travelfusion content.

## How to edit a notification template

1. Log into the admin console.
2. Go to **Notifications → Notification templates**.
3. Choose the notification you would like to edit.
4. Click **EDIT**.

If you have more than one language version, choose the language from the list first, then press **EDIT** again to open the template body.

There are two ways to edit a notification:

### 3a) Simple editation

1. Click the blue window on the task bar menu — the template opens for editing.
2. Click into the template and start editing. You may add, change, or delete text and logos.
3. Save changes when done.

**To add a logo:**
- Choose the area in your template where you want the logo.
- Copy your logo (Ctrl+C) from its source.
- Paste (Ctrl+V) it into your template.
- Save changes.

### 3b) Editation via HTML

If you prefer editing via HTML, click **HTML** on the template task bar menu and go ahead.

> **Tip:** Ask an AI assistant for inspiration if you'd like to improve the visual design of your notifications.

## Notification recipients

You can manage the delivery of every notification by clicking **DELIVERY** on the right side of the template list. Tick or untick the desired recipients and save changes.

**Recipient types:**

- **to customer** — the person who makes a booking / the traveler
- **to dealer** — the agency acting as a dealer
- **to agency** — the owner of the GOL IBE account
- **to configurator** — the system provider (CEE Systems)

**Further settings:**

- **Send itinerary** — manage attachments included with certain notifications.
