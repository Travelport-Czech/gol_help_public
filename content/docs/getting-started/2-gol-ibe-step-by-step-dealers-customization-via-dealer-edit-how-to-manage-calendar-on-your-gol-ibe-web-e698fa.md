# How to manage calendar on your GOL IBE web?

This feature enables you to offer flight tickets from a certain date based on when the booking is made.

- Go to admin console
- Find section **Dealers - Edit**
- Scroll down to the section **Flight search** and find section **Enable search form**
- Choose an option
- Save changes.

</div>

What does that mean?

**Actual day** — you offer the flights even for the day when booking is made

**A day before** — you offer flights from tomorrow.

**2 days before** — you offer flights in 2 days from today.

**3 days before** — you offer flights in 3 days from today.

And so on.
